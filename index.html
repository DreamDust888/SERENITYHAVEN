Our Story

Whispering Cliffs & Time-Honored Luxury

Serenity Haven is perched on a jagged precipice of the Amalfi Coast, rising from a 16th-century former monastery into a timeless sanctuary. Restored with respect for historic architecture, our resort boasts dry stone walls, olive gardens, and sweeping vaults.

Every element is curated to invite quiet reflection. Experience private terraces suspended over the Mediterranean, infinity edges dissolving into the horizon, and tailored services that cater to your highest expectations.

Local Sights & Locations

Convenient day trips located close to the Serenity Haven resort bounds.

Duomo di Amalfi

Historical 9th-century cathedral featuring Arab-Norman architectures, bronze doors, and cloisters.

Distance: 4.2 km Time: 12 mins drive

Positano Cliffs

Picturesque cliffside village famous for pastel houses cascading down to crystal shore grids.

Distance: 16.5 km Time: 35 mins drive

Ravello Gardens

Romantic garden estate terraces at Villa Cimbrone offering panoramic heights of the gulf.

Distance: 8.0 km Time: 20 mins drive
